iPad Artist

Viral is an engineer who always wanted to do something different. Born and raised in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, India, he pursued engineering as part of the conventional educational path. However, since childhood, his true passion has always been rooted in creativity. He was naturally drawn toward creative expression, but like many others, he followed the traditional cycle of education — completing school, enrolling in college, and earning an engineering degree.

The turning point came during his final internship, a phase meant to prepare students for professional life. It was during this time that he realized engineering was not something he wanted to pursue for a lifetime. What he truly wanted was to express ideas through art. His interest in animation developed through watching anime and animated storytelling, which inspired him to explore the power of visual narratives. Recognizing his long-standing creative mindset and unique perspective, he chose to pursue animation as a medium to bring his imagination to life

His ultimate goal is freedom — the freedom to express himself and turn his imaginary worlds into reality through his creative skills. He does not want to be limited to the title of an animator alone; he aspires to be known as an artist — someone who explores different forms of creativity, experiments with ideas, and creates work that stands apart. He aims to tell meaningful stories, grow continuously as an artist, and create work that feels deeply fulfilling. Through his art, he hopes not only to step away from the conventional rat race but also to inspire and bring joy to others through his creations.
